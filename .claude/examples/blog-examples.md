## Pattern: Advocacy/How-To

Comprehensive guides (~1200-1500 words) advocating for practice with actionable steps.

### Example: #NoHobbyists (Cybersecurity Training)

**Source**: [2022-11-06-NoHobbyists.md](../../content/post/2022/2022-11-06-NoHobbyists.md)

**Pattern Notes** (see full post in repository):

**Opening**:
- Problem statement: "We have a talent and knowledge deficit"
- Rally cry: "There should be **#NoHobbyists**"
- Sets up the challenge

**Structure**:
- Context paragraphs explaining problem
- ### [Action Item Header] for each recommendation
- Each section: What to do + How to do it + Why it matters + Examples
- Credits multiple people and resources throughout
- Links to frameworks, tools, resources
- Images illustrating concepts
- "If you take away a single thought..." closing

**Voice**:
- Direct: "We cannot allow..."
- Evidence-based: cites studies, statistics
- Personal experience: "I'm doing this now..."
- Generous credits: names people and organizations

**Length**: ~1400 words

## Content Strategy Notes

### Primary Content: Keynote Presentations
Most blog content is:
- Adapted from keynote presentations
- Distillations of talk concepts
- Companion pieces to conference talks
- Definitions and frameworks from speaking

### Blog Post Types & Lengths

**Short/Focused** (300-500 words):
- Define concept or framework
- Quick thought piece
- Distilled keynote concept
- Example: xOps definition

**Medium** (800-1000 words):
- Single topic deep dive
- Myth-busting
- Framework explanation
- Example: Platform Engineering vs DevOps

**Long/Comprehensive** (1200-1500 words):
- Predictions/trends
- How-to guides
- Advocacy pieces
- Example: #NoHobbyists, 2024 AI Predictions

### Future Enhancements Needed

When expanding blog posts:
- Add more real-world examples and case studies
- Include citations to frameworks/research
- Expand key points with additional context
- Add "common pitfalls" or "challenges" sections
- Include "getting started" actionable takeaways
- Weave in more personal experience stories
